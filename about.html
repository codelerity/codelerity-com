codelerity offer software development, consultancy and advice.

We focus on real-time systems, real-time programming, media, embedded and tools on the JVM.

History

codelerity was founded by Neil C Smith in 2019. Neil has over 20 years of experience working with Java technologies.

Neil is a long-term advocate for free and open-source software, and a member of the Apache Software Foundation. He develops and contributes to a wide range of projects. In particular, he developed PraxisLIVE, a radical open-source Java live programming environment, about which he regularly talks at conferences and user groups.

The technologies in PraxisLIVE form a common thread through the commercial services codelerity provides, and the company continues to support its ongoing development.
